Welcome to Independence Park

Independence Park is a beautiful green space located in the heart of the city. It offers a variety of recreational activities, including walking trails, picnic areas, and playgrounds.

Upcoming Events

Visitor Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking trails.
  • Don't forget sunscreen and water.
  • The park is open from 7AM to 9PM daily.
