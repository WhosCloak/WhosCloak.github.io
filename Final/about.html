History of Independece Park

Independence Park, located at 3449 Pearland Parkway in Pearland, Texas, has been a central recreational hub for the community since its opening in 1976. The 2019 renovations introduced several enhancements to the park, including a new amphitheater with a covered stage, a relocated and updated playground, improved pavilion facilities, reoriented park entrance, and refreshed landscaping.These upgrades have made the park more accommodating for various events and daily activities. Today, Independence Park continues to be a vibrant venue hosting events like the annual Celebration of Freedom on July 4th, the Old Pearland Farmers Market, and various community festivals. Its blend of historical significance and modern amenities makes it a cherished landmark in Pearland.
