Basketball Court

The pavilion at Independence Park doubles as three full-court basketball courts. Beneath the shaded structure, athletes of all ages can have fun shooting hoops here.

Playground

The "Independence" playground system is a commercial playground offering a variety of activities for children of various ages. It features multiple slides, climbing structures, and interactive panels, designed to engage children in both physical and social play.

Trail

a trail within Independence Park, a recently renovated park offering various recreational and event spaces.

Tennis Court

the venue where the sport of tennis is played. It is a firm rectangular surface with a low net stretched across the center. The same surface can be used to play both doubles and singles matches.

Tunnel

The mural in and around the tunnel along the berm boasts vibrant colors while noting what makes Pearland, well, Pearland.
