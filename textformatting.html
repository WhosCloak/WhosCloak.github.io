Lore of Dark Souls III

Dark Souls 3 is the third installment of the Dark Souls series made by FromSoftware.

Story

Game starts as the Ashen One is brought back to life to link the fire once again. Gameplay resembles Dark Souls 1, but with a few improvements. The player needs to defeat Lords of Cinder to offer their souls to Soul of Cinder Linking the First Flame.
Cool fact about Soul of Cinder is, he is the Chosen Undead. The main protagonist of DS1. The hardest boss in the game is The Nameless King Darkeater Midir. A good way to learn more about the lore there is a book called Abyssal Archives: Mythology of Dark Souls by Lokey.
